Please read and follow the instructions.

This is a task to test your memory.

Firstly, you are asked to read a short story and memorize as many details as you can. Then you are asked to answer the questions based on the story.
Your goal is to answer questions as correctly as you can, and as quickly as you can (the time for reading the story will also be counted).
After reading a story and answering its questions, you will see how you rank among our other participants based on how quick and correct you were.

There will be 5 questions for each story, and there are two stories in total. At the end of the task, you'll be directed to a questionnaire. Your prolific ID will be asked at the end of the questionnaire. The whole task would take you around 15 minutes.

Please continue if you understand and would like to participate.
